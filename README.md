## About Me

I am a Bioinformatics Researcher, AI in Healthcare Scientist, and Lecturer with a PhD in Bioinformatics, specialising in the integration of artificial intelligence, multi-omics data, and computational biology for precision medicine and early disease detection.

My research focuses on developing artificial intelligence models, genomic and molecular biomarkers, and machine learning approaches for early cancer detection, particularly lung adenocarcinoma. I combine computational bioinformatics with clinical and healthcare perspectives to support the translation of advanced technologies into real-world healthcare practice.

My expertise includes RNA sequencing, multi-omics analysis, cancer genomics, machine learning, data science, and biomedical research. I am passionate about developing interpretable AI-driven solutions that improve early diagnosis, personalised healthcare, and clinical decision-making.
